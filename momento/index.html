Momento

Bring the memory back.

Momento is a premium tea house built around the relationship between time, memory, and ritual. Inspired by the philosophy that every meaningful experience leaves a lasting trace, the brand curates twelve handcrafted tea leaves, each representing a distinct moment in time. More than a collection of teas, Momento is an archive of moments designed to preserve the memories we create through everyday rituals.

CHALLENGE

Momento required a complete brand identity that reflected its premium positioning while expressing the emotional relationship between time and memory. The challenge was to develop a cohesive visual identity that translated an abstract philosophy into a tangible brand experience across both print and digital applications.

Packaging became the centrepiece of the project. Rather than functioning solely as a container, it was designed to embody the brand itself. Inspired by the passage of time, the circular archive package transforms twelve handcrafted teas into a clock-like collection, reinforcing the brand narrative through both form and function.

The project demanded a unified design system that connected the logo, typography, colour palette, packaging architecture, photography, and brand applications into one consistent experience.
