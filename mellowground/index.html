Mellow Ground

Overview

Have a slow morning.

Mellow Ground is a café designed as a quiet escape from the noise and fast pace of the city. They focus on creating a warm, calm atmosphere where visitors can slow down and enjoy a more relaxed moment. The brand needed a primary logo with variations, strong visual identity, branding strategy, and print-ready packages.

Year

May 2026

Duration

8 Weeks

Type

    Cafe/Beverage Business

Role

Mellow Ground needed a cohesive brand system with a refined visual identity. Based on the brand’s positioning, tone of voice, and intended atmosphere, a complete identity system was developed including the logo, visual language, and key applications to ensure a consistent and recognizable brand presence.

Challenges

The project began with establishing a clear brand direction and visual foundation. It was essential to translate the core idea of “a slow morning” — meaning a quiet escape from the fast pace of the city — into a distinct and recognizable visual language.

This required aligning the conceptual direction with a cohesive identity system, ensuring consistency across the logo, visual elements, and brand applications. Early-stage exploration focused on defining the visual mood and building a shared understanding of the brand’s intent, allowing for a more precise and unified design outcome.

Approach & Key Process

01 Context Discover

Brand Positioning

Defined Mellow Ground as a calm, slow-paced café offering a quiet escape from the fast-paced city.

Competitor Analysis

Reviewed other cafe and beverage branding strategies and identities.

Audiences & Experience

Considered how customers interact with the space, focusing on creating a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere.

Target Audience

Identified key audience groups, expectations, and behaviours, including considerations for food-related interactions.
02 Direction Frame

Concept Definition

Translated the idea of “a slow morning” into a clear visual direction, centred on warmth, calmness, and subtle sophistication.

Visual Direction

Established the overall tone and mood through colour, typography, and composition to support a cohesive brand experience.
03 Design Develop

Moodboard Development

Created three different moodboards to build stronger visual direction.

Logo Syste,

Developed a primary logo and its variations, balancing elegance and readability while reflecting the brand’s calm character.

Typography & Colours

Refined the type and colour system to ensure clarity, consistency, and flexibility across applications.

Brand Applications

Extended the identity across key touchpoints, including packaging, takeaway materials, and environmental elements.
04 Outcome Showcase

Identity System

Delivered a cohesive visual identity system aligned with the brand’s positioning and concept.

Mockups & Presentation

Produced application mockups to demonstrate how the identity functions in real-world contexts.
