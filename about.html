I’m a Designer Who Learns from the Basics and Keeps Improving.

Hi there, I’m Seunghyeon Sydney Yeom, but most people call me Sydney. I’m a graphic designer with two and a half years of experience in UX/UI design and web development. I started in UX/UI and web dev, but was drawn to the fundamentals of design, especially grids, layouts, and typography. It turns out, I have a passion for organizing—whether in design or beyond—and that’s what led me to focus on grid-based design. I’m excited to keep growing and seeing where this path takes me.
