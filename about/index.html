Bio.

Location

Toronto, Canada

I specialize in brand identity design, working independently with startups, studios, and individuals.

I am Sydney Yeom, a branding designer based in Toronto, Canada. Over the past four years, I have worked across digital and physical touchpoints, focusing on brand and visual identity systems through a detail-oriented and thoughtful approach. I enjoy refining ideas into structured visual systems.

Interested in how brands are recalled, remembered, and emotionally connected to everyday life, I explore broader creative perspectives and translate complex ideas into clear and memorable visual experiences.
